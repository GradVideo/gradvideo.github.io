Use this template to create Intranet web pages for your workgroup or project.  You can modify the sample content to add your own information, and you can even change the structure of the web site by adding and removing slides.  The navigation controls are on the slide master.  To change them, on the View menu, point to Master, then choose Slide Master.  To add or remove hyperlinks on text or objects, or to change existing hyperlinks, select the text or object, then choose Hyperlink from the Insert menu.
When you’re finished customizing, delete these notes to save space in your final HTML files.
